The Wayfarer’s Code

A few simple customs followed in every Wayfarer’s Rest across the realm, ensuring safety, fairness, and a warm welcome for all who enter.

The Tavern Code

While each tavern has its own character, all Wayfarer’s Rest locations abide by the same guiding principles.

Respect the House

Guests are expected to treat the tavern, its staff, and fellow patrons with courtesy at all times.

No Blood on the Floor

Disputes must be settled outside the tavern walls. Violence within the house is strictly forbidden.

Mind Your Steel

Weapons are permitted but must remain sheathed unless otherwise stated by the tavern keeper.

The Keeper Decides

The word of the tavern keeper is final in all matters within their walls.
