About the Wayfarer’s Rest

Founded as neutral ground for all travellers, the Wayfarer’s Rest has grown into a network of taverns that span the realm, welcoming merchants, adventurers, and pilgrims alike.

Food & Drink

Hearty meals and well‑kept ales are served at every location, prepared using local ingredients wherever possible.

Rest & Shelter

Clean rooms, safe hearths, and a quiet corner await those in need of rest after a long road.

Atmosphere & Welcome
